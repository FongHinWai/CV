Squircle Tray | Woodworking Application

The interplay between natural wood grain of the pine panels and rastered acrylic base shows both contrast and harmony between nature and man-made. The squircle on the base acrylic mimics ripples from a point gradually taking the shape of its ‘container’.

Wooden Drawer | Woodworking Application

Different ways of joining wood were explored in the process of making. The purpose of this drawer was to contain art materials hence the compartments are long and deep for objects such as paper, brushes, paint and other stationery.

Neck and Back Massager | 3D Printing

BALANCE Stool | Woodworking Application

We lead an increasingly sedentary lifestyle and we tend to spend more time sitting than people in the past. The BALANCE Stool aims to introduce bearable discomfort when our body balance on it to create awareness of one’s sitting posture. Balancing also brings about movement, preventing muscle strain from sitting. This stool also allows user to opt for crossed leg sitting posture.

Paper Structure

ReBoard Rocking Horse | Woodworking Application

Rocking horse is a childhood toy that is expensive to purchase, but children may outgrow of it quickly. Exploring on paper material for such toys provides parents with a low cost and eco-friendly alternative.

Reception Counter | Interior Design

Compact was the keyword for this design. Foldable workspaces and movable pedestals were utilised in this project in order to accommodate the space allowance required by a large 3D printer.

Seating Pod | Interior Design

These seating pods were designed with comfort and privacy in mind. Serving as a temporary respite for users, these pods feature a plush and comfortable sofa with iPad for entertainment and a swivel tray to accommodate snacks. Additional upholstered side panels were installed to provide users with a secure space for a quick respite.

The Hidden Door | Interior Design

Harmony in style and aesthetics with its static counterparts is a key point of this design. Recessed handles were utilised to provide users with hidden touch points while pivot hinges hide away gaps that indicate signs of a door. To reduce bulk of the door, depth of bookshelf door was reduced as compared to the rest of the bookshelf.
